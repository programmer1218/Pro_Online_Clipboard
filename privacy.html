Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Online Clipboard ("we", "our", "the Service") handles information when you use our website. We have designed this Service to be as privacy-friendly as possible.

Information we store

When you send text, an image, or a file using Online Clipboard, that content is temporarily stored on our servers and associated with a randomly generated 6-digit retrieval code. This content is automatically and permanently deleted after 24 hours, or sooner if retrieved and the session expires.

Information we do not collect

We do not require account creation, and we do not knowingly collect names, email addresses, or other personal identifying information through the core clipboard feature. We do not sell or share user content with third parties.

Cookies and advertising

This site may display advertisements served by third-party providers, such as Google AdSense. These providers may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings page at adssettings.google.com.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.

Data security

Content transferred through this Service is stored temporarily and deleted automatically. While we take reasonable steps to protect data in transit and storage, no online service can guarantee absolute security. Please avoid using this tool for highly sensitive information such as passwords, financial details, or confidential documents.

Children's privacy

This Service is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via the GitHub repository associated with this project.
