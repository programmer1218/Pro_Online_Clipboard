About Online Clipboard

Online Clipboard is a free web tool that lets you instantly share text, images, and files between devices — without creating an account, installing an app, or dealing with complicated setup.

Why we built this

Moving a quick note, a screenshot, or a small file from your phone to your laptop (or vice versa) is surprisingly annoying. Email is slow, messaging apps compress images, and cloud storage requires sign-ups and syncing time. Online Clipboard solves this with a simple idea: type or upload something, get a 6-digit code, and enter that code on any other device to retrieve it instantly.

How it works

When you send content, it is temporarily stored and linked to a unique 6-digit code. Anyone who enters that code within 24 hours can retrieve the content. After 24 hours, the content is automatically deleted from our servers. There is no account, no tracking of personal identity, and no long-term storage of your data.

Who it's for

Students transferring notes between a phone and a college computer, developers copying code snippets across machines, designers sending quick image previews, or anyone who just needs to move something from "here" to "there" without friction.

Our commitment

We aim to keep this tool fast, free, and privacy-respecting. If you have feedback or run into issues, feel free to reach out via the contact details on our Privacy Policy page.
